Dear Readers,

This website is built based on a amazon search result for popular books in 2018. The data is scrapped by Parsehub and processed by Open Refine. 

The page aims to provide a database of popular books for keen readers who are looking for new books to read. The user friendly interface allows reader to pick the book that will most likely interest them by the filter function. Some popular works have also been displayed below.

Happy Reading!

Fun Facts

Who are the most popular writers in 2018?

    Through sorting by air table, works written by Stephen King, Neil Gaiman (my personal favourite) and Robert A. Heinlein are the most purchsed on Amazon in 2018. 

Which are the most read genres?  

    Book catagories including contemporary Literature & Fiction, Hard Science Fiction, Magical Realism and Self Esteem & Reliance  are the hot picks!
